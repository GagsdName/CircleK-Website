Operation: Chili for Children

Post by Katherine Carlton, Oct 22, 2016.
Service Event Kiwanis Club

On Friday October 21st, Circle K members arrived at the National Guard Armory to assist the Kiwanis Club of Bloomington in hosting their annual Operation: Chili for Children event. Circle K members helped to cook, serve, and clean up the chili, hot dogs, pizza, and cornbread that was sold to attendees. The event also featured a bounce house, face painting, and coloring station for the children who attended, as well as balloon animals and snow cones. Circle K members diligently manned the bounce house and coloring station and even painted faces!
